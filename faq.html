Frequently Asked Questions.

Do you guarantee visa approval?

No. Visa decisions are made exclusively by the embassy, consulate, or official visa authority. Our role is to provide professional appointment support, clear guidance, and preparation assistance to help you move forward with confidence.

Which countries do you support?

We provide appointment assistance for Netherlands, France, Greece, Belgium, Malta, and other Schengen destinations. Contact us to inquire about support for countries not listed on our website.

What is your response time?

We aim to respond to WhatsApp messages within a few hours during business hours. For complex inquiries, we provide detailed guidance and next steps promptly.

Can I contact you on WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is our primary communication channel and the fastest way to reach the team. Message us at +44 (0) 7766 919721 with your destination and we'll respond promptly.

Do you help with document preparation?

Yes. We provide detailed guidance on common documents required specific to your destination and travel purpose, including checklists and preparation tips.

How do I get started?

Visit our booking page, complete the request form with your details, and submit. Your information will be sent directly to WhatsApp so our team can begin guiding you immediately.

What happens after I submit my request?

The team reviews your request, confirms your destination and travel purpose, checks available support routes, and provides clear next steps. We'll guide you through each stage with professional expertise.
